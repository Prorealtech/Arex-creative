GRAPHIC DESIGNING

Graphic Designers

Create visual concepts, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers. They develop the overall layout and production design for applications.

Illustrator

An artist who creates two dimensional vector images for various verticals, such as fashion design, children's books, magazines, medical manuals, websites, technical designs and advertising.

Multimedia Artist & Animators

Create two-and-three-dimensional models & images that appear to move for website, television, movies, games, and other forms of media.

Motion Graphics Designers

Use visual effects, animation and other cinematic techniques to bring life to their otherwise static graphic designs. They command the highest respect & salary in the industry.

WEB DESIGNING

Layout Designers

Layout designers consider composition and page elements, including font sizes, styling, and spacing and image size and position to create a clear flow of information for the reader.

Web Designers

They create the front end or the visual presentation of the website. Right from the content, the way it looks, the way it works. They translate the mock-up into a fully functional website.

Web Developer

The web developer builds the functionality of the website. The web developer may also build HTML/CSS page templates or may coordinate with the web designer for this task.

SEO Specialist

Analyses, reviews & implements changes to websites so they are rank high in searches. They test and implement marketing techniques, website layouts and advertising for improving search engine ranking.

ANIMATION

3D Modeler

Builds realistic character & prop models in 3D as per the given concept. These models are further used in the animation film development process or game design.

Texturing & Lighting Artist

Adds realistic textures to the models, background and props created by the modeler to suit the requirements of the story line, be it game or film.

Rigging Artist

The process of rigging involves working on the models seamlessly for it to be animation-ready. It involves creating proper joints and pivots for use by the animator in the animation process.

Animator

Gives life to the models by adding motion and turning them into characters for the film or game. The animator can make the movement realistic and harmonious.

VFX

Layout Artist

Implements assets and cameras in a 3D scene. Creates a cinematic vision based on the 2D story-board or the 3D previz.

Rotoscoping Artist

A rotoscoping artist creates different shapes around an object and animate those shapes to match the movement on each frame.

Compositor

Compositor are responsible for constructing the final image by combining layers of previously created material. It is primarily a 2D role within the 3D world of CGI and VFX.

Match-moving Artist

Match-moving artist tracks the camera movement & motion in order to incorporate 3D data or objects into live action footage.
